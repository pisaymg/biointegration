Welcome to Bio-Integration!



Bio-Integration is a Biology project that aims to relate some notable topics in biology to other branches of science like math and chemistry. For example: we may relate metabolic processes to chemical processes and fundamental axioms. We can relate osmosis partly to physics's concept of pressure. Math is a notable tool for epidemiology, statistics makes us infer to biological trends, and sociological studies can be related to epidemiology and heredity.
